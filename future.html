The Future of AI:

  The future of AI is speculative, but it's still in the relm of reasonable speculation. Jobs are already being lost, and will probably continue to be lost. AI art is a negative force for the gifted artists that make commisions for thier art. I suspect unless a law is passed, allowing these jobs to exist, they will cease to exist, and have to find new jobs. Music is the same way.


  There are two possible outcomes in AI for the near future: We regulate it and jobs remain, or there are no laws, and jobs are lost. I bet the soul I sold to Satan, the second one is more likely, as nothing effective never happens for technology law-wise when it's needed, for America at least, China and other dictatorships often ban things before they have the slightest chance of being a threat.


  The future of some AI is unclear, but one thing is, things will continue to change.
